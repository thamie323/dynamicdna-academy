# SDCorp Navigation and Layout Analysis

## Top Banner
- Dark navy background with white text
- Contains: Level 1 B-BBEE contributor message
- Phone: 010 900 3115
- Email: info@sdcorp.co.za
- Social media icons: Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram
- **Two prominent CTA buttons:**
  - "Become a Learner" (red/burgundy button)
  - "Become a Client" (dark/black button)

## Main Navigation Bar
- White background
- Logo on the left
- Navigation items:
  1. HOME
  2. ABOUT SDC
  3. SERVICES
  4. STUDENT HUB
  5. CLIENT INFO
  6. NEWS
  7. CONTACT US

## Layout Style
- **No white spaces between sections**
- Full-width sections that flow seamlessly
- Sections alternate between:
  - Dark navy backgrounds
  - Light/white backgrounds
  - Image backgrounds
- No wave dividers or decorative separators
- Clean, professional corporate look

## Key Design Elements
- Full-width hero slider
- Statistics section with numbers
- Service cards with icons
- News/blog section with article cards
- Footer with multiple columns

## Color Scheme
- Primary: Dark navy blue (#1a2332 or similar)
- Secondary: Red/burgundy for CTAs
- Accent: White text on dark backgrounds
- Clean, high-contrast design
